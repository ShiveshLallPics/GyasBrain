About the Documentary

Gyas Brain

Trinidad and Tobago’s undeniable passion for cars has earned it the title of the Caribbean’s automotive hub.

This documentary explores the island’s vibrant car culture through discussions with enthusiasts. The film takes a deep dive into the perspectives of a drag racers, drop-low specialists , fabricators , garage owners , street racers, off-roaders, circuit racers, sound men and drifters.

Each will share their journey, the challenges of car ownership, and the financial dedication behind their passion.
